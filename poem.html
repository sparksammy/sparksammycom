Samuel Lord - Poem

5/4/17

I believe in my friends.

I believe I can try harder.

As much as I’ve done my best,

As much as I stride through the day,

I might not hate school entirely.

But then again,

I hate the teachers complaining.

I hate the books,

I hate the tests,

I hate the complaining teachers,

I hate the idiots surrounding me.

I also hate these other things:

I believe I hate the notecards,

I believe I hate the assignments,

I believe that school needs repairs,

I believe that school needs to get rid of standardized testing,

I believe that school just needs… ...love.

I believe that I’m running out of faith,

We just have to deal with it.

As much as we despair,

I believe it can get better,

But it probably won’t.

School could get worse.

(Now read it BOTTOM to TOP.)
