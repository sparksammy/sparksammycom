Snaizza

  • Time: 7min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread A slice
Ketchup 3 seconds worth
Cheese Handfull

Step 1: Take a slice of bread

Step 2: Put ketchup on bread, spread around the bread.

Step 3: Take handfull of cheese put it on bread while spreading around the bread

Step 4: Put in microwave for 25 seconds on HIGH.
